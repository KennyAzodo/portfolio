The Man Behind the Legend

Scholar, Artist & Visionary.

Dr. Eziwho Emenike Azunwo — affectionately known as the Academic Rabbi — is a luminary in the realm of academia and a beacon of scholarly excellence. Hailing from the picturesque town of Rundele in Emohua LGA of Rivers State, Nigeria, his journey in academia has been nothing short of extraordinary.

As Associate Professor of Literary Criticism and Playwriting and the immediate past Acting Head of the Department of Theatre and Film Studies at Rivers State University, Dr. Azunwo embodies a profound commitment to education and intellectual inquiry. From his tenure as a lecturer at the University of Port Harcourt to his current role, he has been a catalyst for positive change — shaping the minds of countless students and inspiring the next generation of scholars.

A true Renaissance man, his contributions extend far beyond the classroom. He is a renowned Nigerian playwright, a revered critic, a visionary theorist, and a dedicated advocate for social change — using his platform to shed light on pressing issues such as women empowerment and domestic violence in Nigerian video films.

Beyond his professional achievements, Dr. Azunwo finds strength in family. Married to Dr. (Mrs.) Precious Uchechi Azunwo — herself a lecturer at the University of Port Harcourt — their union is blessed with a son and a daughter.
